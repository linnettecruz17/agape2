MISSION IN ACTION

Our mission in action ministries show Agape Love at it's best by letting a brother or sister give you a hug when you need it most through life hardships. Holding your hand through the difficult times and encouraging you that you will come above it.

Who are we? We are Christ-like individuals who love to give support, show lots of love and provide understanding to those who need it when they are down and out from life circumstances.
