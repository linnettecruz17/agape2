Welcome to AGAPE LOVE

Make an impact by being amazing with your giving heart! Let me start by saying your experience will be like no other ...this is why it's call AGAPE LOVE .

MISSION

AGAPE LOVE let a brother or sister give you a hug when you need it most through life hardships.

Who are we? We are Christ-like individuals who love to give support, show lots of love and provide understanding to those who need it when they are down and out from life circumstances.

WHAT DOES AGAPE MEAN???

Agape (Ancient Greek ἀγάπη, agapē) is a Greco-Christian term referring to love, "the highest form of love, charity" and "the love of God for man and of man for God".
